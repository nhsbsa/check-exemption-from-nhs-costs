Check if you have an exemption from paying NHS costs

An NHS exemption certificate gives you help to pay for your NHS costs, such as prescriptions or dental treatment.

What you can check

Use this service to check if your NHS exemption:

  • is still valid or will expire soon
  • entitles you to free NHS prescriptions
  • entitles you to free NHS dental check ups and treatment

Exemptions you can check

  • prescription prepayment certificate (PPC)
  • medical exemption certificate
  • maternity exemption certificate
  • HC2 certificate (full help through the Low Income Scheme)
  • Tax Credit exemption certificate

Exemptions you can't currently check

  • prescription exemption certificate issued by the Ministry of Defence
  • HC3 certificate (partial help through the Low Income Scheme)

You cannot use this service to check which benefit you're on.
Only some benefits entitle you to free prescriptions

When you don't need to check

You're automatically entitled to free NHS prescriptions and NHS dental treatment if you are:

  • under 16
  • under 19 and in full time education
